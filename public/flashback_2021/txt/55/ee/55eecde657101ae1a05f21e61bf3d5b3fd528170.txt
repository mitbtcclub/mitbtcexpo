Sia is a decentralized cloud storage platform supported by a global ecosystem of data storage hosts and renters. The Sia network is maintained by the Sia Foundation, a non-profit entity.

Skynet is an open protocol and toolkit for creating a better web--one built on decentralized storage and applications. Skynet is layer 2, built on top of the Sia network.
