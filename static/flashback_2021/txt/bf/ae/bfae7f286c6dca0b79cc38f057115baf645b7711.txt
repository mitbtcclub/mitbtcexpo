Miles Anthony is the project lead of Decentral Games, the first DAO-governed metaverse gaming and NFT ecosystem. $DG token holders vote on treasury allocation, economic policy, new game development to grow and strengthen the Decentral Games ecosystem. Miles Anthony is the first to launch this gaming ecosystem concept with a play-to-earn model. His work is supported by his belief that true digital ownership lies in blockchain technology. Prior to his interest in crypto and starting Decentral Games, Miles founded an e-commerce company while still studying in UCLA.
